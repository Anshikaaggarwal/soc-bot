About

Finivesta, the finance and economics society of IGDTUW, is dedicated to enhancing financial awareness and literacy among women while fostering a keen interest in both personal and professional financial matters, as well as their effective management. We are committed to creating a vibrant women community of finance and economics enthusiasts who want to advance their knowledge and understanding of the field. To help amazing women like you become financially independent by sharing with you the 'must-know' basics of finance and investment. No matter which branch or course you belong to, this topic is important for everyone.
